Interactive Charts

Chart 1: Sentiment Scores by Character and Chapter

This interactive bar chart features a drop-down menu that displays the sentiment scores of each character in the novel. You can choose to display the sentiment scores for the entire novel or for each individual chapter. By selecting a specific chapter, the graph displays the sentiment scores of each character in that chapter.

Chart 2: Sentiment Scores by Character and Chapter (Line Graph)

An interactive line graph with drop-down menu. You can choose the characters from the menu and the graph shows the sentiment score of the character in each chapter and fluctuations and trend during the novel.
